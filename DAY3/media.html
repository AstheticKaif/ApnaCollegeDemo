About SST

Scaler School of Technology (SST) is a Bangalore-based leading private institution that was established by eminent Hyderabad IITians Anshuman Singh and Abhimanyu Saxena with the objective to develop and transform students into such a competent workforce that can bring stellar changes in the tech industry. The institute offers a fully residential and Integrated 4 year B.Sc + M.Sc Degree course, admission for which is granted to students based on their marks in last year’s qualifying exam.
